The story of the Good Samaritan

The Good Samaritan is a parable from the New Testiment

Jesus told this parable in response to a question from a lawyer, who wanted to test him and justify himself. The lawyer asked Jesus, "Who is my neighbor?" He probably expected Jesus to give a narrow definition that would exclude the Samaritans and other despised groups. But Jesus surprised him by telling a story that challenged his prejudices and assumptions.

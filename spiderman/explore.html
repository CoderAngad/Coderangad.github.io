Marvel's

Spider-Man: No Way Home

Spider-Man: No Way Home is a 2021 superhero movie that follows Peter Parker after his identity as Spider-Man is exposed to the world by Mysterio. He asks Doctor Strange to cast a spell to make everyone forget, but the spell goes wrong and opens up the multiverse, bringing in villains from other realities. Peter has to face these enemies and learn what it means to be Spider-Man.

Marvel's

Spider-man: Far from Home

Spider-Man: Far from Home is a superhero film that follows Peter Parker as he goes on a school trip to Europe with his friends. He is recruited by Nick Fury and a mysterious hero named Mysterio to fight the Elementals, powerful creatures that threaten the world. However, Peter soon learns that not everything is as it seems, and he must face a new enemy that manipulates illusions.

Marvel & Sony's

Spider-man: Into the Spider-verse

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse is an animated superhero film that features different versions of Spider-Man from parallel universes. The main character is Miles Morales, a teenager who gets bitten by a radioactive spider and gains spider-like abilities. He meets other Spider-People, such as Peter Parker, Gwen Stacy, Spider-Ham, Spider-Man Noir, and Peni Parker, who help him stop the villainous Kingpin from using a machine called the Collider to destroy reality and bring back his family.

Sony's

The Amazing Spider-man 2

The Amazing Spider-Man 2 is a superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character Spider-Man. It follows the adventures of Peter Parker, who struggles to balance his personal life with his responsibilities as Spider-Man. He faces a new threat from Electro, a former Oscorp employee who can manipulate electricity, and his old friend Harry Osborn, who becomes the Green Goblin. Peter also uncovers the truth about his parents' death and their connection to Oscorp.
